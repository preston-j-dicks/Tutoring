How to pick from this list

You don't need all 7. Start with the one that solves your biggest current bottleneck:

  • If your biggest problem is organization: Notion first.
  • If you're drowning in content creation: Jasper or RepurposeAI.
  • If you're losing leads: HubSpot immediately, it's free.
  • If you want to launch a course: Thinkific.
  • If you want to expand to audio/video: ElevenLabs.

The compounding effect of building on solid tools is real. One good system that you actually use beats five sophisticated ones you abandon after two weeks.
